About miminions.ai

We're building the future of autonomous AI agents. Our mission is to make AI agents accessible, safe, and powerful for developers worldwide.

Our Vision

To democratize AI agent development and create a world where autonomous AI systems can safely and effectively assist in various tasks. We believe that agentic AI will transform how we work, create, and solve problems.

Our Mission

Providing developers with the tools and framework they need to build reliable, safe, and powerful AI agents. We're committed to open-source development, community collaboration, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible with autonomous AI.

What We Do

  • Build open-source AI agent frameworks
  • Develop multi-agent orchestration systems
  • Create safety mechanisms for autonomous AI
  • Provide tools for enterprise AI deployment
